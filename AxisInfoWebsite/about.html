About Us

AXIS INFOLINE PVT. LTD., having its registered office at 196/16B, East of Kailash New Delhi, is a group of professionals engaged mainly in the trading of IT/Office automation/ Telecom/ Audio-Visual products. Since its incorporation in 2001 AXIS INFOLINE PVT. LTD, has come a long way to have a base of more than 500 satisfied customers. AXIS INFOLINE also undertakes third party maintenance of Desktops/Servers/Peripherals and Networking Products.

Corporate Philosophy

Being aware of the saying – “An organization is only as good as its people” , we at AXIS INFOLINE, have developed a team consisting of professionals who are highly qualified, have vast working experience in IT industry and who have graduated from prestigious institutes like IITs, RECs and premium management institutes.

Customer Orientation

To provide the best of the products available in the industry to its customers and to cut down the costs due to the existence of intermediaries, AXIS INFOLINE has adopted customer-oriented tie-ups with IT giants like ACER, IBM, HP, Lenovo, DELL, Cisco and MICROSOFT for selling their products in the Indian markets during the last couple of years. AXIS INFOLINE has also entered into a tie-up with TVSE /CANON, EPSON, XEROX, SONY, EPSON, LG and SHARP for selling their IT & Office automation products. ACER, IBM and DELL have appointed AXIS INFOLINE as their Business Partner/Service Provider for their products in addition to being authorized reseller.

Maintenance Management

A team of qualified engineers headed by the Maintenance Manager takes care of “after-sales-support” and the “third party maintenance of Desktops/Laptops/ Servers/Printers etc.”. The team is competent enough to handle different products on various platforms like Microsoft Windows, Windows Server Software/ Linux/ Ubuntu/ MAC/ UNIX etc.

In-House Service

Our TRC (Test Repair Center) is well equipped with diagnostic instruments/tools and is managed by trained engineers and is headed by an experienced Manager-Support. TRC takes care of the field defectives in-house.
